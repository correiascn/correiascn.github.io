About

Welcome to my site!

My passion for information security ignited during the early stages of my IT career when I recognized the critical role of safeguarding an organization's assets within its network. Over the course of my career, I've been deeply involved in supporting and fortifying all aspects of IT operations. This hands-on experience has not only equipped me with a comprehensive understanding of business risks but has also emphasized the significance of confidentiality and empowered me to advocate for robust security measures.

My academic journey began at the University of Guelph, where I majored in Computer Science. This strong foundation armed me with the technical prowess and problem-solving skills that have been invaluable in my IT and information security career.

The ever-evolving landscape of technology constantly challenges me to expand my knowledge and adapt to new threats. I stay dedicated to continuous learning with online courses and certifications, as well as experimenting with different technologies in my home lab. I also work on projects as a demonstration of my skills and knowledge.
